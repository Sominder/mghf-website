What is PMAY 2.0?

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana 2.0 (Urban) is the Government of India's initiative to provide affordable housing to all eligible urban households. Under the Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS), eligible beneficiaries receive an interest subsidy on their home loan, significantly reducing the cost of borrowing.

MGHF is registered as a Primary Lending Institution (PLI) for PMAY, enabling us to directly process PMAY subsidy claims for eligible borrowers.

Who Can Apply

PMAY 2.0 Eligibility Criteria

🏠 EWS — Economically Weaker Section

Annual household income up to ₹3 Lakh. Subsidy of 6.5% on loans up to ₹6 Lakh. Max benefit: ₹2.67 Lakh.

📊 LIG — Low Income Group

Annual household income ₹3–6 Lakh. Subsidy of 6.5% on loans up to ₹6 Lakh. Max benefit: ₹2.67 Lakh.

💼 MIG — Middle Income Group

MIG-I (₹6–12 L annual income): 4% subsidy on ₹9 L. MIG-II (₹12–18 L): 3% subsidy on ₹12 L.

Key Conditions

🚫

No Existing Pucca House

Beneficiary or any family member must not own a pucca (permanent) house anywhere in India

💍

Women Ownership Preferred

For EWS/LIG, property must be in the name of female member or jointly with male member

🏙️

Urban Areas Only

Property must be located in a statutory town or urban agglomeration as per census

✅

First-Time Beneficiary

Applicant must not have availed PMAY or any central government housing subsidy earlier
