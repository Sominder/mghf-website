What is Empanelment?

Pre-Approved Projects for Faster Home Loans

MGHF Empanelment means your housing project is vetted, legally verified, and pre-approved for home loan disbursements. When a buyer purchases a unit in your project and applies for an MGHF home loan, the legal and technical evaluation of the project is already done — making approval dramatically faster.

This gives your buyers a competitive advantage and makes your project more attractive in the market.

Who Can Apply

Who Can Get Empanelled?

🏗️

Real Estate Developers

Registered developers with approved layouts, RERA-registered projects, and clear title. Applicable for residential apartments and townships.

🏡

Individual Builders

Individual landowners or small builders developing row houses, bungalow projects, or plotted developments with necessary approvals.

🏛️

Housing Societies & Co-ops

Registered housing societies undertaking redevelopment or new construction on society land with requisite government approvals.

Documentation

Documents Required for Empanelment

Developer / Builder Documents +
  • Developer Registration Certificate
  • PAN and GST Registration
  • Audited financial statements (last 3 years)
  • Previous project completion certificates
  • Director / Proprietor KYC documents
Project / Land Documents +
  • Title Deed & Chain Documents (7/12, Index II)
  • RERA Registration Certificate
  • Approved Building Plan / Layout
  • Development Agreement (if applicable)
  • Environmental Clearance (if applicable)
  • No Objection Certificates (NOC) from relevant authorities
Other Supporting Documents +
  • Brochure / project details
  • Unit pricing and schedule of payments
  • Progress photographs (for under-construction projects)
  • Sanctioned plan copies
