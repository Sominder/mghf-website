Simple Process

Get Your Loan in 3 Easy Steps

From application to disbursement, our entire process is designed to be fast, transparent, and hassle-free.

1

Apply Online or at Branch

Fill a simple application on our website or visit our office. Upload documents from your phone — no physical copies needed initially.

2

Verification & Approval

Our team evaluates your profile and property. You receive real-time updates. Most applications are processed within 48–72 hours.

3

Loan Disbursement

Upon approval, funds are disbursed directly to the seller or builder. For construction loans, staged disbursement as per progress.
