FAQs

Partner FAQs

What is the minimum qualification to become an MGHF partner? +
  • Must be a resident Indian individual or registered entity
  • Prior experience in financial products, real estate, or financial advisory preferred
  • Valid KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN)
  • NBFC/DSA registration (if applicable)
How much can I earn as an MGHF partner? +
  • Commission is based on loan type and disbursement amount
  • Home loan commissions typically range 0.5%–1% of disbursed amount
  • Additional performance bonuses for top-referring partners
  • Contact our partner team for detailed commission structure
How long does the partner onboarding take? +
  • Initial registration: Submit form below (5 minutes)
  • Document verification: 24–48 hours
  • Agreement signing and activation: 1–2 business days
  • Total: Typically active within 3–5 business days
When and how are commissions paid? +
  • Commissions are calculated on actual disbursement amount
  • Paid monthly via bank transfer / NEFT
  • TDS deducted as per applicable rates
  • Detailed commission statements shared with every payout
