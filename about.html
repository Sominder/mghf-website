About Micro Green Housing Finance

We exist to make homeownership a reality for every Indian family — digitally accessible, financially inclusive, and sustainably built.

Our Mission

Democratizing & Digitising Housing Finance

Micro Green Housing Finance Private Limited (MGHF) was established with a singular purpose: to bridge the massive housing finance gap for underserved and overlooked segments of India — the self-employed, the informal income earners, and first-time home buyers in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.

We are a Non-Deposit Taking Housing Finance Company (ND-HFC), registered and regulated by the National Housing Bank (NHB). Our leadership team brings over 100 years of combined experience from India's leading banks, NBFCs, and financial institutions.

At MGHF, we believe that technology can make housing finance truly inclusive. Our 100% digital onboarding process means a customer in a small town can apply, submit documents, and track their loan entirely from their smartphone — no bank visits, no paperwork queues.

Green Finance

Financing a Sustainable Future

The "Green" in Micro Green isn't just a name — it's a commitment. We actively encourage borrowers to choose eco-friendly construction materials and energy-efficient designs.

Our sustainable housing initiative provides additional support to customers building homes with green building practices, reduced carbon footprint, and renewable energy features.

  • 🌱 Preference for IGBC/GRIHA certified green buildings
  • ☀️ Support for solar panel integration financing
  • 💧 Rainwater harvesting & water-efficient designs encouraged
  • 🏗️ Fly ash bricks, AAC blocks, and sustainable materials promoted
