Customer Awareness

Important information to protect you from fraud and help you make informed financial decisions.

⚠️

Beware of Fraudulent Activities

Micro Green Housing Finance (MGHF) NEVER:

  • Asks for advance payment, security deposit, or processing fees in cash
  • Requests payment to personal bank accounts of employees or agents
  • Promises guaranteed loan approval without verification
  • Asks for OTP, net banking password, or debit/credit card details
  • Offers fake job positions requiring fees or deposits
  • Contacts you through unverified WhatsApp numbers not on our website
If you receive any such communication, immediately report to:

Know Your Rights

Customer Information & Rights

💡 Know Before You Borrow

Understand your loan terms, interest rates (floating vs fixed), prepayment charges, and total cost before signing any agreement.

📑 Read Your Sanction Letter

Your sanction letter has all key terms — interest rate, tenure, processing fee, prepayment conditions. Read it carefully before acceptance.

🔒 Keep Your Documents Safe

Never share original property documents with unauthorized persons. MGHF holds originals only during loan tenure as per signed agreement.

📊 Monitor Your CIBIL Score

Regular EMI payments improve your credit score. Late payments or defaults can impact future loan eligibility. Check score periodically.

📞 Know Escalation Channels

If you face any issue, escalate through our Grievance Redressal mechanism. We have a time-bound resolution process.

🏛️ Regulatory Protection

As an NHB-regulated HFC, you're protected by NHB guidelines. You can also approach NHB's grievance portal at nhb.org.in if needed.

KYC Requirements

KYC & AML Policy

As per Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA) and RBI/NHB guidelines, MGHF is required to complete KYC (Know Your Customer) verification for all customers. This protects both you and the financial system.

Officially Valid Documents (OVDs) accepted for KYC:

  • ✓ Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • ✓ PAN Card (mandatory)
  • ✓ Passport
  • ✓ Voter ID Card
  • ✓ Driving License
  • ✓ NREGA Card
