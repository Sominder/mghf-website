Most Important Terms & Conditions (MITC)

Key terms and conditions you must know before availing any loan from Micro Green Housing Finance.

Read these terms carefully before signing any loan agreement with MGHF.

📊 Interest Rate

Floating rate linked to MGHF's benchmark lending rate. Rate can change as per RBI/NHB policy and market conditions. You will be notified 30 days in advance of any change.

📅 EMI & Repayment

EMIs are due on the date specified in the sanction letter. Bounce charges apply for failed NACH debit. Late payment attracts penal interest as per agreement.

💰 Foreclosure

Floating rate home loans: No prepayment charges for individual borrowers. Fixed rate loans: Charges may apply as per loan agreement terms.

📋 Processing Fee

Processing fee is collected upfront and is non-refundable in case of voluntary withdrawal of application after sanction.

🏠 Property Insurance

Property insurance is mandatory for the loan tenure. Borrower may choose their own insurer. Life insurance linked to loan is recommended but not mandatory.

📄 Original Documents

MGHF holds original title documents during loan tenure as security. These will be returned on full repayment within 30 days.
