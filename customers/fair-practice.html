Fair Practice Code

MGHF's commitment to fair, transparent, and ethical lending practices in compliance with NHB guidelines.

The following Fair Practice Code (FPC) outlines the standards of fairness and transparency that Micro Green Housing Finance Pvt Ltd (MGHF) commits to in its dealings with customers, in line with the National Housing Bank's (NHB) guidelines on Fair Practices Code for Housing Finance Companies.

1. Loan Applications & Processing

  • ✓ Loan application forms will be clear and comprehensive in required information
  • ✓ MGHF will acknowledge receipt of loan applications
  • ✓ Reasons for rejection of loan applications will be communicated in writing
  • ✓ No discrimination on grounds of sex, caste, religion, or gender in lending decisions

2. Loan Appraisal & Terms

  • ✓ Sanction letter will mention all terms including rate of interest, method of application, EMI, and all charges
  • ✓ Interest rates will be disclosed upfront; any change to floating rate will be communicated in advance
  • ✓ Pre-payment charges and conditions will be clearly stated in the loan agreement

3. Disbursement

  • ✓ Loan disbursements will be as per agreed terms in the sanction letter
  • ✓ Any conditions post-disbursement will be clearly specified in advance

4. Post-Disbursement Supervision

  • ✓ MGHF will not interfere in borrower's affairs unless necessary for loan recovery
  • ✓ Recovery practices will adhere to legal norms with no coercive methods

5. Grievance Redressal

  • ✓ A structured Grievance Redressal Mechanism is in place (see Grievance Redressal page)
  • ✓ Board-approved Grievance Redressal Officer is available for escalation

This code is reviewed and approved by the Board of Directors of MGHF and is in compliance with NHB Master Directions on Fair Practices Code for HFCs.
