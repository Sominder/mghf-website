FAQs

E-Mandate FAQs

What is E-Mandate / NACH? +
  • NACH (National Automated Clearing House) is an RBI-regulated system
  • It allows automatic debit of EMI from your bank account on the due date
  • Eliminates manual transfers and risk of missed EMIs
  • Fully secure and regulated by NPCI
How do I register for E-Mandate? +
  • You will receive an eNACH registration link on your registered email/mobile
  • Click the link and authenticate via net banking or debit card OTP
  • Mandate is registered with your bank within 2–5 business days
  • Alternatively, visit our office for physical NACH form submission
Can I cancel my mandate after loan closure? +
  • Yes, upon loan closure MGHF will initiate mandate cancellation
  • You can also request cancellation through your bank's net banking
  • Cancellation typically takes 3–5 business days to reflect
What if my account has insufficient balance on EMI date? +
  • The debit will fail and may attract a bounce charge from your bank and MGHF
  • Repeated bounces may impact your CIBIL score
  • Please maintain sufficient balance in your account on EMI due dates
  • Contact our team immediately if you anticipate a shortfall
