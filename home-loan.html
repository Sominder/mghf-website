Documents Required

Simple Documentation

Upload all documents digitally from your phone. No courier, no physical submission for initial processing.

Identity & Address Proof +
  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Passport / Voter ID / Driving License
  • Utility Bills (Electricity / Water / Gas) — for address proof
  • Rent Agreement (if applicable)
Income Proof — Salaried +
  • Latest 3 months' salary slips
  • Form 16 (last 2 years)
  • Last 6 months' bank statements
  • Employer appointment letter / offer letter
  • Last 2 years' ITR (if applicable)
Income Proof — Self-Employed +
  • Last 3 years' ITR with computation
  • Last 3 years' financial statements (CA certified)
  • Last 6 months' bank statements
  • Business registration / GST registration
  • Shop & Establishment Certificate
Property Documents +
  • Sale Deed / Agreement to Sale
  • Title Documents (7/12 extract, Index II)
  • Approved plan / layout (for construction)
  • Occupancy Certificate (for ready property)
  • No Encumbrance Certificate
  • Property Tax receipts (last 3 years)
